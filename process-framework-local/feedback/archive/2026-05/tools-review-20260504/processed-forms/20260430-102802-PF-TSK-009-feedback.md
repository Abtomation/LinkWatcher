Reading current state instead of trusting the IMP wording was the right move. The "Data-Driven Validation" criterion is what prompted the verification.
- **Task routing guidance in Step 2**: Made the "is this PF-TSK-009 scope?" decision trivial for all 3 IMPs (all were content-update or script-modification work to existing artifacts; none required Structure Change or Framework Extension delegation).
