## Improvement Suggestions

### What worked well
- **Step 11 skeleton-then-IMPs ordering** gave a stable `-SourceLink` filename for the entire 10-IMP batch. Filename `tools-review-20260505-080644.md` was used uniformly without retrofitting.
- **`-BatchFile` mode for IMP registration** registered 10 IMPs in a single ~3-second invocation. No file-lock contention this round (contrast with PF-FEE-1089's mid-batch split).
- **Sequential careful reading** through 13 forms in 4 task groups produced a coherent theme map across PF-TSK-009 (9 forms) — patterns stabilized by form 5-7 but novel observations continued sporadically through the remainder.
- **Deduplication callout at Step 12 (now also-checking tech-debt-tracking.md per PF-IMP-712)** kept registration noise down — verified all proposed IMPs against both `process-improvement-tracking.md` and `technical-debt-tracking.md` before filing.
- **Explicit-filename `mv` instead of glob** for archival (PF-IMP-699 explicit-filename move list) — archive count matched move-list length on first verification.
- **"Observe narrow fix before broadening" memory rule** filtered ~10 single-occurrence observations from the IMP list, keeping the proposed batch focused on recurring/clearly-actionable items.

Recovered by prepending absolute `cd`. Not a Tools Review-specific issue but worth noting since archive-then-extract is a canonical Step 14→15 sequence.
- **Single-IMP `New-ProcessImprovement.ps1` "Next Steps:" addendum** still emits the verbose-output noise pattern (PF-IMP-721 filed this session to broaden PF-IMP-700's recipe).

### Specific suggestions
