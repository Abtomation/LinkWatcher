## Improvement Suggestions

### What worked well
- **Step 11 skeleton-then-IMPs ordering** gave a stable `-SourceLink` filename for the entire batch.
- **`-BatchFile` mode for IMP registration** registered 16 IMPs across 2 invocations (split forced by file-lock); much faster than 17 individual calls would have been.
- **Sequential form reading discipline** caught novel observations across the 26-form PF-TSK-009 group even after the pattern set stabilized by form 5-7.
- **Deduplication callout at Step 12** kept registration noise down — pre-checked that PF-IMP-671/584/657/682 already covered related issues before filing.
- **Explicit-filename `mv` instead of glob** for archival avoided the over-archive incident that PF-FEE-1055/1056 documented.

### What could be improved
- **Mid-batch file-lock contention** when LinkWatcher reads process-improvement-tracking.md during script writes. This session lost PF-IMP-702 to the lock (acceptable per ID-gaps policy) but added ~1 min recovery work. PF-FEE-986 previously flagged this; a retry-on-IOException loop in `New-ProcessImprovement.ps1 -BatchFile` would harden the workflow.
- **Step 12 deduplication only mentions process-improvement-tracking.md** — for tool-class IMPs that may already exist as tech debt, agents must independently check tech-debt-tracking.md. PF-IMP-712 filed for this.
- **Step 14 archive concurrency safeguard absent** — same-date Tools Review sessions both write to `tools-review-YYYYMMDD/processed-forms/`. PF-IMP-699 filed for this. (Did not hit it this session because no parallel session was running, but the gap remains.)

### Specific suggestions
- Consider adding a retry-on-IOException loop to `New-ProcessImprovement.ps1 -BatchFile` Set-Content. With LinkWatcher in the background, brief contention is expected during long batches.
- When the active forms folder contains a NEW form (created during the Tools Review session by a parallel agent), the task does not explicitly say to keep it active. I inferred from "DO NOT Archive newly created" but a one-line clarification ("if forms appear in feedback-forms/ during your session that were not in your inventory at Checkpoint 1, leave them active") would make the rule mechanical.
