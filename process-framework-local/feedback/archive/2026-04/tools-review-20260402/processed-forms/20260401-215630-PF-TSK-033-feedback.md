### What worked well
- Parallelized session execution significantly reduced wall-clock time for validation rounds
- The New-ValidationReport.ps1 script reliably handled ID assignment and tracking updates
- The tech debt quality gate prevented low-value items from cluttering the debt tracker
- The workflow cohort grouping (WF-003 for this batch) provided meaningful cross-feature analysis context

### What could be improved
- Concurrent file edits by LinkWatcher caused repeated read/edit failures on the tracking file, requiring fallback to sed
