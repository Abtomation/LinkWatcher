## Improvement Suggestions

### What worked well
- **Step 11 grep sweep + 11b "Read surrounding context" sub-step**: The single highest-value step in this session. Turned IMP-613 from a 4-file edit (per IMP description) into a complete 19-edit cleanup. Without 11b, I'd have skimmed the additional hits as "just legends" and missed the context-map mermaid edges, the bootstrap script's auditableStatuses array, and the test-tracking-template peer file.
- **Step 2 Minimum-Viability gate**: Both IMP-595 (chose Approach B over new ChangeType+template) and IMP-613 (chose docs-removal over script-add) hinged on the explicit Min-Viability comparison at Step 5. This gate prevents complexity creep by force.
- **Step 5 Routing table**: Direct precedent lookup for IMP-618 (IMP-612 → Start-BatchAudit.ps1 cleanup, same vintage) made the PF-TSK-009 vs PF-TSK-014 routing decision data-driven, not stylistic.
- **Mid-flight scope expansion checkpoint** (introduced ad-hoc in IMP-613): When Step 11 grep sweep doubled the scope, presenting the expansion at a fresh checkpoint before continuing was the right call. The user could have aborted to reconsider Approach A vs B; instead approved expansion. This pattern should be codified.

### What could be improved
- **Mid-flight scope-expansion handling is implicit**: PF-TSK-009 has Step 11 (grep sweep) and Step 13 (review-with-hit-list) but no explicit checkpoint for "Step 11 sweep revealed scope materially larger than Step 10b plan". I improvised one in IMP-613; would benefit from being a named step or explicit guidance.
- **New-FeedbackForm.ps1 fixed 2-tool template**: Sessions with 3+ tools require manual section cloning. PF-IMP-582 (in flight) addresses this.
- **Update-ProcessImprovement.ps1 `-ValidationNotes` length**: My IMP-613 and IMP-618 ValidationNotes were close to 1000 chars each; the script accepted them but agents in past sessions hit the 500-char Description cap on creation. Worth checking whether ValidationNotes has the same cap pre-emptively.

### Specific suggestions
- **Add explicit "Scope-Expansion Checkpoint" step** between PF-TSK-009 Step 11 (grep sweep) and Step 13 (review): "If Step 11 grep sweep reveals additional hits beyond the Step 10b approved scope, present the expanded scope to the human partner before continuing. Do not silently expand or stop work prematurely." Would have served as my IMP-613 mid-flight checkpoint formally.
- **Step 11 hit-list table**: Currently the task definition shows the table format with 4 columns (File, Line, Context Summary, Action). Could add a 5th implied column or rule: "If table grows beyond Step 10b file count by ≥50%, treat as scope expansion → checkpoint."
