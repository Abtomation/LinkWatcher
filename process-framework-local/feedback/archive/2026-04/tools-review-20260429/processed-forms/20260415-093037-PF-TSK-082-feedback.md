## Improvement Suggestions

### What worked well
- Gitignore check pattern caught `.linkwatcher.lock` immediately
- Safety check for sensitive files provides good guardrail
- LinkWatcher stop/restart steps prevent background interference
- Clear decision points that defer to human partner (e.g., unstaging files)

### What could be improved
- No guidance for pre-commit hook failure recovery (re-stage + retry cycle)
- Step 5 (stop LinkWatcher) comes after staging, leaving a window where LinkWatcher could modify staged files

### Specific suggestions
- Add a note after Step 6 about pre-commit hook failures: "If hooks fail but auto-fix files, re-stage with `git add .` and retry the commit"
- Consider moving Step 5 (stop LinkWatcher) before Step 2 (staging) to eliminate the race window
