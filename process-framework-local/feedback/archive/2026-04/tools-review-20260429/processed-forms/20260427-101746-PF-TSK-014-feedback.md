## Improvement Suggestions

### What worked well
- **Lightweight scope criteria are well-calibrated** — the 5-criterion classification table in Step 1 gives a fast unambiguous answer. Even when scope expanded to 4 files mid-execution, the criteria still held without forcing escalation to Full.
- **Lightweight skips the proposal/state-tracking ceremony** that would be overkill for ≤5-file changes. Direct-execution path matched the actual work.
- **Mid-task scope expansion is handled gracefully** — when I discovered Update-TechnicalDebtFromAssessment.ps1 and again when the corruption fixes (lines 126/132/154) were folded in, the existing Step 5 checkpoint was the natural place to surface findings and let the user decide.
- **The orchestrator principle (Step 11)** — "PF-TSK-014 plans, tracks, coordinates structure changes; does NOT perform specialized work inline" — was a useful guardrail. For Lightweight, the specialized work was simple enough to do inline; for Full it would route correctly.
- **PF-IMP-599 Delegated → Completed lifecycle** worked smoothly. The handoff from PF-TSK-009 to PF-TSK-014 was clean.

### What could be improved
- **Validate-StateTracking.ps1 Surface 6 crash blocks the completion gate** — the Lightweight checklist demands "0 errors across all surfaces" but the script can't get past surface 6 due to PF-IMP-590. A continue-on-error mode (or surface independence) would make this gate satisfiable.
- **No explicit guidance on what to do when Validate-StateTracking is blocked by an unrelated bug** — the task definition assumes it always runs; an "if blocked by known issue X, document in feedback form and proceed" clause would help.
- **The Lightweight checklist's smoke-test expectations are implicit** — for script changes (PF-TSK-014 changed two scripts here), `-WhatIf` smoke-testing and `PSParser::Tokenize` parse-check are clearly best practice but aren't called out as expected verification steps. Could be added to the Step 6 Verify section.

### Specific suggestions
- Add a note to PF-TSK-014 Lightweight Step 6 (Verify): "For script changes, perform a `-WhatIf` smoke-test invocation and a `PSParser::Tokenize` parse-check before checkpointing."
- Add a note to PF-TSK-014 Step 8 / completion checklist: "If Validate-StateTracking.ps1 fails due to a known pre-existing issue (e.g., PF-IMP-590), document the affected surface in the feedback form and proceed; do not attempt to fix the unrelated issue inline."
- Consider whether mid-task scope expansion (3 files → 4 files) should trigger a re-scope check — the current process correctly handled it via human approval at Step 5, but a formal "Scope expansion detected: re-evaluate Lightweight criteria" prompt could be useful.
