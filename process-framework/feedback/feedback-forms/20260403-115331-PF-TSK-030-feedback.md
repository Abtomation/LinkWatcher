- Two-checkpoint structure in PF-TSK-030 keeps human partner appropriately informed

### What could be improved
- New-TestAuditReport.ps1 needs re-audit support (-Force flag or versioned output)
- PF-TSK-030 needs a "re-audit fast path" for unchanged code
