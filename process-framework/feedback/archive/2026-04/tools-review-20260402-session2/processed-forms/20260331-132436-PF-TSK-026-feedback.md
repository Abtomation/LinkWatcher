## Improvement Suggestions

### What worked well
- Concept document with detailed per-session roadmap eliminated session-start planning overhead
- Lightweight approach (modify existing, don't create new) kept 25 items manageable in one session
- Checkpoint-per-batch pattern caught issues early (e.g., step renumbering in Bug Fixing task)
- Update-WorkflowTracking.ps1 composition pattern (called by existing scripts) avoids new workflow entry points

### What could be improved
- Step renumbering when inserting steps into existing tasks is error-prone and tedious — 8 sequential edits for Bug Fixing alone
- The concept document's E2E Status derivation from e2e-test-tracking.md Workflow Milestone section assumes a specific table format that may drift

### Specific suggestions
- Consider adding a "Step Insertion Checklist" note to PF-TSK-026 for when modifications involve step renumbering: (1) insert step, (2) renumber all subsequent, (3) grep for internal step references (e.g., "Step 12"), (4) update cross-references
- The Update-WorkflowTracking.ps1 script should have its E2E status parsing validated by Surface 7 once implemented
