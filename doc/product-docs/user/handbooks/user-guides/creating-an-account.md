# Creating an Account

## Overview

This handbook walks you through the process of creating a new account in the BreakoutBuddies application. Creating an account allows you to save your game progress, compete on leaderboards, and connect with friends.

## Prerequisites

Before creating an account, make sure you have:
- A valid email address
- A stable internet connection
- The latest version of the BreakoutBuddies app installed

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Launch the Application

Open the BreakoutBuddies application on your device. If this is your first time opening the app, you'll be taken to the welcome screen.

!<!-- [Welcome Screen](../../../../assets/images/welcome-screen.png) - File not found -->

### 2. Navigate to Registration

From the welcome screen, tap the "Create Account" button. If you're on the login screen, tap "Don't have an account? Register" at the bottom of the screen.

!<!-- [Registration Navigation](../../../../assets/images/registration-navigation.png) - File not found -->

### 3. Enter Your Information

Fill in the registration form with your information:
- Email address: Enter a valid email address that you have access to
- Password: Create a strong password (at least 6 characters)
- Display Name: Choose a name that will be visible to other players
- Date of Birth: Enter your date of birth for age verification

!<!-- [Registration Form](../../../../assets/images/registration-form.png) - File not found -->

### 4. Accept Terms and Conditions

Read the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, then check the box to indicate your acceptance.

!<!-- [Terms and Conditions](/doc/assets/images/terms-conditions.png) - File not found -->

### 5. Complete Registration

Tap the "Create Account" button to submit your registration. The app will process your information and create your account.

!<!-- [Create Account Button](/doc/assets/images/create-account-button.png) - File not found -->

### 6. Verify Your Email

After registration, you'll receive a verification email at the address you provided. Open the email and click the verification link to confirm your account.

!<!-- [Email Verification](/doc/assets/images/email-verification.png) - File not found -->

### 7. Set Up Your Profile

Once your email is verified, you'll be prompted to set up your profile. You can:
- Upload a profile picture
- Customize your avatar
- Set your preferences
- Connect with friends

!<!-- [Profile Setup](/doc/assets/images/profile-setup.png) - File not found -->

## Tips and Best Practices

- Use a unique, strong password that you don't use for other accounts
- Make sure to verify your email to unlock all features
- Choose a display name that doesn't reveal personal information
- Complete your profile setup to enhance your gaming experience
- Enable two-factor authentication for additional security

## Troubleshooting

### Registration Email Not Received

**Problem:** You completed the registration form but didn't receive a verification email.

**Solution:**
1. Check your spam or junk folder
2. Verify that you entered your email address correctly
3. Wait a few minutes as emails can sometimes be delayed
4. Use the "Resend Verification Email" option on the login screen

### Password Requirements

**Problem:** Your password is being rejected during registration.

**Solution:**
1. Ensure your password is at least 6 characters long
2. Include a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters
3. Avoid commonly used passwords
4. Try a completely new password

## FAQs

**Q: Can I use the same email for multiple accounts?**

A: No, each account requires a unique email address.

**Q: Can I change my display name later?**

A: Yes, you can change your display name in the profile settings.

**Q: Is my personal information shared with other players?**

A: Only your display name and profile picture are visible to other players. Your email and other personal information remain private.
